Landfall Games (Developer/Publisher)

About Studio

(From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia)

Landfall Games (commonly known as simply Landfall) is a Swedish independent video game developer and publisher based in Stockholm. It was founded in 2015 and is best known for developing or publishing video games such as Clustertruck (2016), Totally Accurate Battlegrounds (2018), Totally Accurate Battle Simulator (2021), Content Warning (2024) and PEAK (2025).

Landfall is known for publishing their games on April Fools' Day.The studio specializes on physics-based games. On their official website, they describe themselves with a quote of Petter Henriksson, the COO of the company: "Small studio staying small. Makes physics games, also other games, sometimes funny. Doing well, now funds things sometimes, only cool stuff."
