Welcome to SOFS! (Safe Overlays for Streamers)

This is a Wiki site where streamers, and content creators alike can find info about where to place their overlays for certain games without spoilers!

 

Want to find a page? Just hit search at the top and look for a game, or use the sidebar navigation.

 

If you know a game that is not added yet, please leave a comment with the Game Name on the relative page.

(For example, suggesting Clair Obscur: Expedition 33, would be a comment on the 2025 Releases page.)

 

The SOFS Wiki is free and completely open-source!
